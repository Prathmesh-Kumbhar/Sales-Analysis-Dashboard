# Sales Analysis Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard using Power BI)

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance across different regions, products, and payment methods. The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve revenue and profitability.

## Dataset Used

* Sales Dataset (Excel)

## Questions (KPIs)

* What is the Total Sales generated?
* What is the Total Profit?
* What is the Profit Margin (%)?
* How many Total Orders were placed?
* What is the Average Order Value?
* Which region generated the highest sales?
* Which products contributed the most to sales?
* Which payment method was used the most?
* How do sales and profit change over time?
* Which salesperson generated the highest revenue?

## Process

* Cleaned and validated the dataset using Power Query.
* Removed duplicate and missing values.
* Created relationships and data model.
* Developed DAX measures for KPIs.
* Built interactive visualizations and slicers.
* Designed an interactive dashboard for business analysis.

## Project Insights

* Total Sales exceeded **4.5M** during the analysis period.
* North America generated the highest sales revenue.
* Printer and Tablet were among the top-selling products.
* Online and Credit Card payments contributed the highest sales.
* Sales and profit showed seasonal trends across different months.

## Final Conclusion
The dashboard enables businesses to monitor sales performance, identify high-performing regions and products, and evaluate payment trends. These insights support better inventory planning, sales strategies, and overall business decision-making.
